Shingeki no kyojin Season Finale

-Attack on titan Final Season 4

The fourth and final season of the Attack on Titan anime television series
titled Attack on Titan: The Final Season, is produced by MAPPA, chief
directed by Jun Shishido, and directed by Yūichirō Hayashi, replacing
Tetsurō Araki and Masashikoizuka respectively. Scriptwriter Hiroshi Seko
took over the series composition from Yasuko Kobayashi, and Tomohiro
Kishi replaced Kyōji Asano as character designerdue to the series
switching production studios.The score is directed by Masafumi Mima and
composed by Hiroyuki Sawano and Kohta Yamamoto.[1] The opening theme
song is "My War" (僕の戦争, "Boku no Sensō") performed by Shinsei
Kamattechan, and the ending theme song is "Shock" (衝撃, "Shōgeki")
performed by Yūko Andō.
